<p><p>The brake power boosters market primarily consists of three types: single diaphragm boosters, dual diaphragm boosters, and others. Single diaphragm boosters utilize a single membrane to create vacuum pressure, suitable for smaller vehicles and basic braking systems. Dual diaphragm boosters employ two diaphragms for enhanced braking force and are commonly used in larger or performance vehicles, providing better responsiveness. The "others" segment includes specialized designs and innovations, catering to specific automotive needs or non-conventional braking systems.</p></p>
