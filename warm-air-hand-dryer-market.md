<p><p>The warm air hand dryer market is segmented into two main types: manual switch and fully automatic. Manual switch dryers require users to activate the device by pressing a button or pulling a lever, offering control over operation. In contrast, fully automatic dryers utilize sensors to detect hand presence, activating automatically for a touch-free experience. This hands-free functionality promotes hygiene and convenience, making fully automatic models increasingly popular in public restrooms, while manual switch options remain cost-effective and straightforward.</p></p>
