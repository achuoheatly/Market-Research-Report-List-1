<p><strong>The 5G Infrastructure Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Femtocell</li><li>Pico Cell</li><li>Micro Cell</li><li>Macro Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G infrastructure market consists of various cell types designed to improve network coverage and capacity. Femtocells are small, low-power nodes ideal for enhancing indoor coverage in homes or small businesses. Picocells are slightly larger, serving specific public areas like offices or shopping malls. Microcells cover larger outdoor areas, bridging gaps between picocells and macro cells. Lastly, macro cells are high-power base stations that provide extensive coverage over wide-ranging regions, forming the backbone of the 5G network.</p></p>
