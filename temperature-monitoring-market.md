<p><p>The temperature monitoring market is divided into two main types: non-contact and contact temperature sensors. Non-contact temperature sensors utilize infrared technology to measure surface temperatures without physical contact, making them ideal for quick assessments and hazardous environments. In contrast, contact temperature sensors require direct contact with the object to obtain accurate readings, commonly used in industrial applications, HVAC systems, and medical settings. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring safety, efficiency, and reliability across various industries.</p></p>
