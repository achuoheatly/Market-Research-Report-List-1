<p><p>Non-contact ultrasonic sensors are devices that utilize ultrasonic waves to measure distance, detect objects, or monitor levels without physical contact. These sensors operate by emitting high-frequency sound waves, which reflect off nearby objects and return to the sensor, allowing for precise measurement of distance. <p><p>The Non-Contact Ultrasonic Sensors market is categorized into two main types: Open Type and Sealed Type. Open Type sensors are designed for applications with exposure to environmental elements, allowing for easy airflow and sound wave emission, making them suitable for general measuring tasks. In contrast, Sealed Type sensors are encased in protective housings, offering enhanced durability and reliability in harsh environments, such as chemicals or extreme temperatures, ensuring accurate measurements without risk of damage.</p></p>
