<p><strong>The Gas Inserts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vent-Free</li><li>Direct Vent</li><li>Natural Vent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas inserts are categorized into three main types based on their venting systems. Vent-Free gas inserts operate without a chimney, using oxygen from the room and producing minimal emissions, making them ideal for well-insulated spaces. Direct Vent inserts draw air from outside for combustion, ensuring higher efficiency and safety by venting exhaust outside through a sealed system. Natural Vent inserts rely on traditional chimneys to exhaust gases and intake air naturally, providing a classic heating option, but may be less efficient than the other types.</p></p>
