<p><strong>The Magnetic Overload Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic</li><li>Dashpot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic overload relay market primarily consists of two types: electronic and dashpot relays. Electronic overload relays employ advanced circuitry for precise monitoring and protection against motor overheating, offering adjustable settings and enhanced features like communication capabilities. In contrast, dashpot overload relays utilize a mechanical system to detect motor overload through a bimetallic element, providing a simpler and more traditional approach. Both types serve to protect electric motors from damage due to excessive current, but they differ in technology and application suitability.</p></p>
