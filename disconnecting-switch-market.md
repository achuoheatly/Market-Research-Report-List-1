<p><p>The Disconnecting Switch market is divided into two main types: Fused and Non-Fused Disconnecting Switches. Fused Disconnecting Switches include built-in fuses that provide overcurrent protection along with disconnection capabilities, making them suitable for applications that require added safety against electrical faults. Non-Fused Disconnecting Switches, on the other hand, do not have internal fuses, offering a simpler design for applications where overcurrent protection is handled separately. Both types are essential for ensuring safe electrical isolation in various industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
